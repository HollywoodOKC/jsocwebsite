FAQs

How active is this unit?

JSOC has operations that run everyday. Operators in our server are required to be active when they can be, we understand life comes first.

How do I get promoted?

Promotions are done by TIR (Time in Rank) as well as several other factors. Operators who show initiative and the desire to progress will be promoted.

What kind of operations are conducted?

JSOC as of right now has two units, 1st SFOD-D and the CIA. Depending on the mission requirements, it could be a JTF (Joint Task Force) or a unit on its own. Delta handles most Direct Action and Hostage Rescue operations plus more. The CIA usually handles intelligence gathering and recon, they also work directly with Delta if the mission calls for it.
